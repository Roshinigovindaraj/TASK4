Our core value is independence


Eega mission is to help consumers make better choices for their health.
In doing so, it aims to drive manufacturers to offer better products.





No ads


     Eega is a completely ad-free.
Brands cannot pay Eega to advertise
             their products.

No Influence


Scores and recommendations are
    obtained independently, with
absolutely no influence from outside
      brands or manufacturers.

Data protection


Eega does not process or sell user
data. All user data remains strictly
                 confidential.
